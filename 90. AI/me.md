## My Obsidian Mindset

This vault follows this flow:

```text
Inbox → Source Materials → Main Notes → Tags → MOCs
```

Meaning:

1. `01. Inbox/` captures quick and unfinished thoughts.
2. `02. Source Materials/` captures what I consume.
3. `03. Main Notes/` captures what I understand.
4. `04. Tags/` stores tags as notes.
5. `05. MOCs/` stores maps of larger topics.
6. `50. Attachments/` stores files.
7. `99. Templates/` stores templates.
8. `90. AI/` stores AI instructions, drafts, and history.

## AI Boundaries

AI drafts must start in:

`90. AI/generated/`

Saved AI conversations must go in:

`90. AI/history/`

Do not put AI-generated content directly into `03. Main Notes/` unless I explicitly ask.

Do not overwrite my original notes.

Do not delete or move files without permission.
